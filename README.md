## What Is This?

A 14-slide strategy presentation for the **Head of Business & IT Enablement** role at Ivoclar Vivadent. It covers:

- **Executive Overview** — Cost Center to Value Engine transformation
- **50/30/20 Portfolio** — Strategic balance across growth, health, and innovation
- **4-Pillar Strategy** — Demand, Priority, AI, Process (Why / What / How)
- **TBM & FinOps** — Financial transparency and unit economics
- **The Airport Metaphor** — IT without a control tower
- **Pareto Analysis** — Fix two things, solve 80% of the chaos
- **Enablement Engine** — Front Door, governance, demand-to-value process
- **AI Orchestration** — Sandbox-to-Scale with FinOps gate
- **Shadow Demand** — Managing global IT outside headquarters
- **365-Day Roadmap** — Listen, Launch, Stabilize
- **Stakeholder Management** — Co-creation, not dictation
- **Success Scorecard** — 4 KPIs in plain English

## How to Use

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `→` or `Space` | Next slide |
| `←` | Previous slide |
| `N` | Toggle speaker notes |
| `F` | Full screen |
| Swipe left/right | Navigate (touch devices) |

Click any item in the sidebar to jump directly to a slide.
